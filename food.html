Lisa likes...

Church of Sweden New York

Image Credit
Midtown East Baked Goods Cafe Lunch Scandinavian

5 E. 48th St, New York, NY 10017
212-832-8443
M-F 12 P.M.-6 P.M., Sa-Su 12 P.M.-4 P.M.

A cozy delight nestled in the heart of midtown Manhattan, the library cafe at the Church of Sweden New York is a sweet respite from the hustle of a lively city. Visitors can indulge in traditional Swedish fare such as open-faced sandwiches called smörgås, with anchovies, dill and eggs on dense brown bread. While sitting among the classics of playwright August Strindberg and the adored author Selma Lagerlof, the aroma of freshly made cinnamon buns topped with crumbly sugar crystals beckon for attention. They are soft and delectable and worth the trip.
